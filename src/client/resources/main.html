About S2x

S2x is a custom client for Call of Duty®: WWII, created as an independent community project. This project is provided for personal and educational use. Please use it responsibly.

S2x does not condone piracy or the unauthorized distribution of game files. You must own a legitimate Steam copy of Call of Duty®: WWII in order to use this client.

S2x is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Activision, Sledgehammer Games, Call of Duty, or any related publishers, developers, or trademark holders. Call of Duty® and Call of Duty®: WWII are trademarks of their respective owners.

The name S2x is a homage to the former X Labs projects and their work in the Call of Duty modding community. S2x is not affiliated with X Labs, and any references are made out of respect for their influence on this project.
